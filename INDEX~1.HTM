The verdict

A clean two horse race

With your 45 minute transfer rule applied, Creta Royal drops out (a full hour from Heraklion). That leaves two strong options, both rated 8.5 out of 10.

Mitsis Royal Mare · ~£2,650 (~£2,810 with daytime flights)

Best value and the best Italian and sushi within budget. Mature, calm crowd, but not strictly adults only.

Ikaros Beach · ~£3,417 (~£3,575 with daytime flights)

Guaranteed adults only and child free, very good food, shortest transfer, for roughly £700 to £850 more.

Also considered near Heraklion: Mitsis Selection Laguna (Anissaras, the sister resort, about £3,261 all in, scores 9.0, same 25 minute transfer) is a livelier family resort with a waterpark and a nightly kids' mini-disco, and its board includes only one à la carte booking a week, so it is busier with children and less generous on à la carte than Royal Mare. Mitsis Selection Rinela (Kokkini Hani) is the group's big aquapark resort and the most family focused option of all, and is sold out for this week (around £4,400 on nearby dates). Nana Princess scores a remarkable 9.9 but is about £7,000+ for the room alone. Abaton Island is cheaper (about £2,450 room only) but scores 8.8 and is only part adults only. None beats the two above for a calm, adults focused, foodie stay under budget. The other Mitsis Selection hotels (Blue Domes, Alila) are on Kos and Rhodes, not Crete.

Before you book

Room type

Prices are the lead in garden view rooms. Sea view or bungalow upgrades add roughly £56 to £171pp, and Ikaros has swim up options. There is plenty of headroom at Mitsis for a sea view.

Drinks

Neither is premium spirits all day. Mitsis is "selected local and some premium brands, at certain times"; Ikaros is "selected hot, soft and alcoholic". If you enjoy good wine and cocktails, confirm the brands and serving hours.

Location and vibe

Mitsis at Anissaras is the calmer setting. Ikaros sits on a private beach but is at Malia, Crete's party capital, so the town strip is loud. Both are away from the strip, but worth knowing.

Kids in July

Mitsis is not strictly adults only and July is school holidays, so expect some families, though a mature, quiet crowd. Ikaros is guaranteed adults only.

Extra costs to budget for

Spa treatments, any à la carte beyond the included allowance, motorised watersports, excursions and tips sit on top. Your budget headroom comfortably covers these.

Entry admin (Greece)

The EU's biometric Entry/Exit System went live on 10 April 2026, so expect fingerprint and photo checks at the border; allow extra time. ETIAS (the €20 authorisation) is not required yet, expected late 2026 at the earliest. Each passport must be issued within the last 10 years and valid for at least 3 months after you return. Arrange travel insurance and a free GHIC card.
